Mario Adams

Visual artist(mostly) from the island that denies that it's an island. Obsessed with human nature, fascinated by cameras and oil paintings. Most of my work reflects a part of me that always feels a nostalgia of an unknown source, heroic feelings, love - its evils and virtues - , and the sadness of finding exactly what you were looking for after a long journey to get it. Creating right from my soul, shouting my thoughts without filters. I've found this to be not just the most effective way for me to bring something to life, but also a way to feel what's around me, things that I would normally disregard or ignore. As a result, also a way of healing and learning how to live. Additionally I believe that creation should be fun and cool, and not just an academic performance or a technical practice.
